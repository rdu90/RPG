# Ingredients

- **Explore:** You send scouts to find habitable worlds and uncover galaxy secrets.
- **Expand:** You settle planets and grow a ragtag band of survivors into a galaxy-spanning empire.
- **Exploit:** You manage a complex economy, navigate a "sprawling technology tree" with many technologies, and use **spies and espionage** for an advantage.
- **Exterminate:** You engage in tactical fleet combat against hostile aliens and rival human factions, utilizing planetary bombardments and invasions

- **Core Loop:** This is a single-player (or sometimes competitive multiplayer) game where the objective is to move between star systems on a map of the galaxy to buy and sell merchandise to make money and gain influence
- **Merchandise:** The player trades various resources, normal, illegal, exotic, immoral. What they trade is sort of their "alignment"

- **Economics:** Star systems have different levels of economic development, which influences their needs, what they produce, and the resulting market prices. **Haggling** over prices is a central part of the gameplay.
- **Interface:** The game is entirely **low to no graphics** and features a galaxy map represented by coordinate nodes
